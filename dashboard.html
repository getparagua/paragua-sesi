Validation: "what usually happens next"

In the past, when SESI regimes looked like this, how much did prices typically move afterwards? The chart shows the median 7-day max move (€/MWh) by regime state (threshold = 80).

How to read
  • Each day is classified into a regime (Calm / Latent risk / Realized stress / Full crisis).
  • Then: what was the biggest move from today's price in the next 7 days?
  • The bar shows the median of those "biggest moves" across history.

Historical conditional statistics — descriptive, not forecasts.
