Data Access

v0.1: visual benchmark only

SESI is currently published as a visual market benchmark. At this stage, SESI is not distributed as a downloadable dataset.

Why no CSV / API in v0.1?

This is a deliberate product choice. SESI is designed as a market signal and communication tool, not as a raw data dump.

Future availability

Historical datasets and programmatic access (API) may be introduced in a future release. Extended history and feeds may be granted selectively for research or institutional use.

If you are interested in early access, please get in touch.
